\#Project\_9 - E-Commerce Orders Analysis:



\## Overview

==>This project analyzes an e-commerce orders dataset using Python and Pandas. The goal is to help an online store understand sales performance, customer spending, product trends, and payment behavior by analyzing delivered orders.



\#Features

\- Removed duplicate records

\- Calculated order value before discount

\- Calculated discount amount and final revenue

\- Analyzed delivered order revenue

\- Found highest and lowest value orders

\- Displayed top 5 highest-value orders

\- City-wise revenue analysis

\- Category-wise revenue analysis

\- Most preferred payment method

\- Most sold product

\- Top 5 customers by spending

\- Total discount given

\- Order with the highest discount

\- Delivery, cancellation, and return percentages

\- Customers spending above and below average

\- Most popular product category



\#Technologies Used

\- Python

\- Pandas
