\#Project 6 – Customer Orders Analysis



\#Overview

==>This project analyzes customer order data for an online store using Pandas. The goal is to clean the dataset, calculate revenue after discounts, and generate business insights to support management decisions.



\## Features

\- Remove duplicate records

\- Calculate Revenue

\- Calculate Discount Amount

\- Calculate Final Revenue

\- Total Revenue after Discount

\- Average Order Value

\- Highest Spending Customer

\- Highest Revenue Product

\- Revenue by City

\- Revenue by Category

\- Most Preferred Payment Method

\- Top 5 Customers

\- Top 5 Products

\- Highest Discount Order

\- Customers Spending Above Average

\- Customers Spending Below Average

\- Export Cleaned Dataset



\#Technologies Used

\- Python

\- Pandas
