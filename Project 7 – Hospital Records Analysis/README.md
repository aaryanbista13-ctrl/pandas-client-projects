\####### Project 7 – Hospital Records Analysis



\## Overview



==>This project analyzes hospital patient records using Python and Pandas. It simulates a real client request where hospital management wants business insights from patient and billing data.





\#Features

\- Remove duplicate patient records

\- Calculate Hospital Bill

\- Calculate Insurance Discount

\- Calculate Final Bill

\- Calculate Total Revenue

\- Calculate Average Hospital Bill

\- Department-wise Revenue Analysis

\- Highest Revenue Department

\- Average Bill by Department

\- Number of Patients in Each Department

\- Doctor Treating the Most Patients

\- Revenue Generated by Each Doctor

\- Average Patient Age by Department

\- Most Common Disease

\- Top 5 Highest Hospital Bills

\- Highest Insurance Discount

\- Patients Spending Above Average

\- Patients Spending Below Average

\- Export Cleaned Dataset





\#Technologies Used

\- Python

\- Pandas





\#Files

\- hospital\_records.csv

\- cleaned\_hospital\_records.csv

\- hospital\_records\_analysis.py





\#Skills Demonstrated

\- Data Cleaning

\- Feature Engineering

\- Business Analysis

\- GroupBy Operations

\- Aggregations

\- Sorting

\- Boolean Filtering

\- Exporting Cleaned Data
